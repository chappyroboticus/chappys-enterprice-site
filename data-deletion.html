Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: 2026-06-27

This page explains how to request deletion of data related to Chappy's Enter Price, Chappy's Car Stories, Chappy's Cartifacts or a connected Meta/Facebook app.

How to request deletion

  1. Email chappyroboticus@gmail.com with the subject line Data Deletion Request.
  2. Include the platform involved, for example Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, TikTok or website contact.
  3. Include enough information for us to locate the data, such as your username/profile URL or the email address you used to contact us.

What happens next

We will review the request, verify that it relates to data we control, and delete or anonymize eligible data unless retention is required for security, legal compliance or platform integrity.

Meta app data

If a Meta/Facebook app is connected, the app is intended for Chappy-owned Page publishing and management. If any user data has been stored through that app, deletion can be requested using the email above.

Expected response time

We aim to respond within 30 days.
