Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-27

Chappy's Enter Price is the public home for Chappy's Car Stories and Chappy's Cartifacts. This policy explains what information we collect and how it is used.

Information we collect

  • Basic contact information if you email us or contact us directly.
  • Public social-platform interactions, such as comments, follows or messages you choose to send through platforms like Facebook, Instagram, TikTok or YouTube.
  • Technical website data that may be provided by hosting, analytics, security or platform services, such as IP address, browser type, device information and request logs.

How we use information

  • To respond to messages and support requests.
  • To operate, secure and improve the website and social content workflow.
  • To publish and manage Chappy's Car Stories content on owned social pages where permission has been granted.

Meta / Facebook data

If a Meta app is used, it is intended to manage Chappy-owned Pages and publish Chappy-approved content. We do not sell Meta user data. We only request permissions needed for the stated publishing or page-management purpose.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be processed by hosting providers, email providers, social platforms or other service providers needed to operate the site and content workflow.

Retention and deletion

We keep information only as long as needed for the purposes above, legal requirements or platform security. You can request deletion by emailing chappyroboticus@gmail.com.

Contact

For privacy questions, email chappyroboticus@gmail.com.
